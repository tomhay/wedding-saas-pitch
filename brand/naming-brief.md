# Brand Naming Brief

## The Challenge
"BaliLove" is too location-specific for a global SaaS platform. Need a new brand that:
- Works globally (not tied to Bali)
- Conveys wedding/hospitality
- Sounds professional for B2B sales to hotels
- Has available domain (.com preferred)
- Is memorable and easy to spell

## Brand Positioning

### What We Are
- Wedding technology platform for hotel teams
- Enables hotels to run world-class wedding operations
- Guest-facing + staff-facing solution

### Brand Attributes
- Professional (selling to hotel executives)
- Modern (technology-forward)
- Warm (weddings are emotional)
- Global (destination weddings worldwide)
- Trusted (handling important life events)

### Target Audience
1. **Primary:** Hotel wedding coordinators and sales teams
2. **Secondary:** Hotel GMs and revenue managers
3. **Tertiary:** Couples and their guests
