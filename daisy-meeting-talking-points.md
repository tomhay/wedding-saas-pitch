# Meeting Talking Points: Daisy (Oshom Bali)

**Prepared for:** Tom
**Meeting with:** Daisy, Oshom Bali representative
**Purpose:** Confirm product pricing and partnership terms for BaliLove platform

---

## 1. Opening (2 min)

- Thank Daisy for the time and express genuine excitement about the partnership opportunity with Oshom.
- Acknowledge Oshom's positioning: 5-star boutique hotel, 18 rooms, incredible location in Nuanu Creative City, Tabanan.

> "Today I want to walk you through exactly how your products will appear in our platform and confirm the pricing together."

- Set expectations: "By the end of this meeting, I'd love to have agreed pricing so we can get Oshom live on the platform."

---

## 2. The Platform Demo (5 min)

- Pull up the wishlist mockup.
- Explain how couples interact with it:
  - They browse and build their wedding package by selecting products.
  - Products are organized by category: **Venue, F&B, Accommodation, Services**.
  - Each product shows pricing, has quantity controls, and is date-aware (seasonal pricing, blocked dates).
- Emphasize: "This is what the couple sees. Clean, transparent, easy to customise."

## 3. Walk Through Each Product (15 min)

### Product 1: Hotel Buyout (18 rooms)

| | Per Room/Night | 2-Night Total | 3-Night Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Oshom net | 4,000,000 | | |
| Buy (incl 21% tax) | 4,840,000 | 174,240,000 | 261,360,000 |
| Sell (couple pays) | | 204,988,235 | 307,482,353 |

**Key argument if Daisy pushes back on price:**

- The net rate of 4,000,000/room/night matches Oshom's 2026 budget ADR of 4,028,398.
- Wedding night revenue = **87.1M/night** vs normal night revenue of 44.1M (2026 budget).
- That is **1.97x a normal night's revenue**.
- In weak months (e.g., November at 29.8% occupancy): a single wedding night delivers roughly **10x normal revenue**.

> "You're not discounting. You're guaranteeing a full buyout at your budget rate, and total revenue nearly doubles per night."

| | Per Room/Night |
|---|---|

> "The rate per room is lower, but total room revenue per night exceeds your budget because every room is full."

## 6. Partnership Terms (5 min)

Confirm each point clearly:

- **Commission:** 15% built into the pricing gap between buy and sell. Not invoiced separately -- Daisy never sees a commission line item.
- **Non-exclusive:** Both BaliLove and Oshom are free to work with other partners.
- **No upfront cost to Oshom:** BaliLove invests in marketing, sales, and wedding planning.
- **Payment flow:** Couple pays BaliLove at milestones. BaliLove pays Oshom at each corresponding milestone.
- **Exit terms:** 60/90-day notice to end the partnership. No lock-in.
- **Partnership agreement:** Template is ready for Daisy's review.

> "There's no risk or cost to Oshom. We invest, we bring the couples, we handle the planning. You deliver the experience."

## 7. Questions to Confirm with Daisy

Work through each of these. Note her answers directly.

1. **Buy prices acceptable?** Especially the 4,000,000 net/room/night for the buyout.
2. **Venue fee:** Flat 40M buy, or should this be a sliding scale (e.g., lower for off-peak)?
3. **Guest room rate:** Flat 3,500,000 sell year-round, or seasonal? (The app supports date-range overrides.)
4. **F&B tiers:** Which to launch with? (Recommend Standard + Premium.)
5. **Room allocation:** 17 vs 18 guest rooms -- confirm 1 room is held for the couple as part of the buyout?
6. **Guest room revenue:** Does it offset the buyout cost, or is it additive on top?
7. **Blocked dates:** Any dates Oshom will not allow weddings?
8. **Payment terms:** 50% deposit + balance 60 days prior -- does this still apply?
