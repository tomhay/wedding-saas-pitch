## Key Insights

### 1. OpCo/PropCo Structure
- Recommends combining OpCo and PropCo in a way that if you invest in one, it trickles down to the other
- De-risk the PropCo investment through the OpCo's track record
- The OpCo will run the wedding business, the PropCo will own venues (land, buildings, permanent installations)
- PropCo generates income through rent paid under a formal lease agreement with the OpCo

### 2. De-Risk via Lease Guarantee (Key Insight)
- "The simplest way to raise for a PropCo in this environment is to pitch as a real estate investment, but a guaranteed tenant"
- Sign a minimum guarantee / baseline lease from OpCo to PropCo
- "Long-term leases are gold in real estate"
- Could do a 2-3 year guarantee per property
- This de-risks the investment far more than anything else
- Asian investors are very risk averse — guaranteed lease solves this

### 3. Management Company Structure
- Create a management company that sits between OpCo and PropCo
- Tom as GP/managing partner
- Fee stack: acquisition fees, asset management fees, property management fees
- Carry/promote on all profits above hurdle
- Management company is the OpCo/PropCo middleman

### 4. Deal-by-Deal Approach
- Given Bali DD timelines (1 year for one property), deal-by-deal SPVs might be easier to start
- Prove it with 1-2 locations, then expand
- Even as SPVs, still connect to the OpCo for de-risk

### 5. Deck Feedback
- "Your deck is actually quite good"
- Needs better "who you are today" section upfront
- Story order should be: Who we are → What we've done → Where we're going → How we get there
- Too much data dump, needs more story
- Supporting documents can go in data room, not the pitch deck
- "Create the opportunity and the value" — don't give everything in the deck
- The alpha needs to be very clear

### 6. Investor Targeting
- Different investor types for OpCo vs PropCo
- OpCo investors = startup/VC types who want scale (Hawaii, Japan, Europe)
- PropCo investors = real estate investors who want de-risked yield
- Can leverage OpCo to sell PropCo

### 7. Next Steps

- Email: laurent.grill@jll.com
- Suggested a few more passes on the deck before sharing
