# Competitive Landscape Analysis

## Direct Competitors

### Tripleseat
- **Website:** https://tripleseat.com
- **Focus:** General venue CRM and event management
- **Pricing:** Not publicly listed (enterprise sales)
- **Strengths:** 2,500+ venues, 18,000 users, strong hotel presence
- **Gap:** Not wedding-specific, no guest self-service portal, generic event focus

### Event Temple
- **Website:** https://eventtemple.com
- **Focus:** Hotel and venue sales CRM
- **Pricing:** From $249/month
- **Strengths:** #1 trending in category, 25+ countries, hotel-focused
- **Gap:** Sales CRM only, no guest-facing tools, no wedding-specific workflows

### Planning Pod
- **Website:** https://planningpod.com
- **Focus:** Venue management with 40+ integrated tools
- **Pricing:** Varies by plan
- **Strengths:** Comprehensive toolset, QuickBooks/Salesforce integrations
- **Gap:** Designed for independent venues, not hotel wedding teams

### Perfect Venue
- **Website:** https://perfectvenue.com
- **Focus:** Private event management
- **Pricing:** Not listed
- **Strengths:** Modern UI, Stripe integration, AI features
- **Gap:** General events, not wedding-specialized

### Releventful
- **Website:** https://releventful.com
- **Focus:** Multi-venue management
- **Pricing:** Not listed
- **Strengths:** 25+ tools, good for venue groups
- **Gap:** Not wedding-specific, no guest portal

## Adjacent Competitors

### Chkdin
- **Website:** https://chkdin.com
- **Focus:** Wedding guest check-in and management
- **Strengths:** Hotel accommodation dashboard, RSVP with travel info capture
- **Gap:** Point solution only, not full planning platform

### TTO (That's The One)
- **Website:** https://thatstheone.com
- **Focus:** Professional wedding planners
- **Strengths:** Built for planners, guest logistics tools
- **Gap:** For independent planners, not venue/hotel teams

### SquadTrip
- **Website:** https://squadtrip.com
- **Focus:** Destination wedding travel coordination
- **Strengths:** Group travel, payments, RSVPs
- **Gap:** Travel-focused only, not venue operations

### Zola / The Knot
- **Focus:** Couple-facing wedding planning
- **Strengths:** Massive consumer brand, registries
- **Gap:** Consumer product, venues are just listings, no B2B tools

## Our Unique Value Proposition

**Only platform that:**
1. Was built FROM a real wedding planning operation (not theorized)
2. Designed specifically for hotel wedding teams (not generic venues)
3. Includes guest-facing self-service (couples + guests can book directly)
4. Has marketplace potential (cross-venue inventory for ancillary revenue)
5. Uses low-friction commission pricing (no software budget approval needed)
