## The Model

Oshom is a boutique hotel with **18 rooms** (17 guest rooms + 1 master/couple suite) and **in-house F&B**.

Couples buy three products:

1. **Venue Buyout** — exclusive-use of the property for 2 or 3 nights
2. **Food & Beverage** — catering for 80 guests (tiered)
3. **Guest Rooms** — 17 rooms booked by wedding guests (offsets buyout cost for the couple)

BaliLove commission = **15%** = spread between sell (couple pays) and buy (venue keeps).

## Product 1: Venue Buyout

| Package | Sell (Couple Pays) | Buy (Oshom Keeps) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 2-Night Buyout | 119,000,000 IDR | 101,150,000 IDR |
| 3-Night Buyout | 154,000,000 IDR | 130,900,000 IDR |

### How we got here

**Question to confirm:** How was the 119M / 154M sell price derived?

Looking at the page, the buyout and guest rooms are presented as the same number (119M for 2 nights), which suggests:

- 17 rooms × 3,500,000/room/night × 2 nights = **119,000,000 IDR** (sell)
- 17 rooms × 3,500,000/room/night × 3 nights = **178,500,000 IDR** (sell for rooms)
- But the venue buyout for 3 nights is **154,000,000** — a different number

So the "venue buyout" and "guest rooms" are presented as **separate line items** that happen to overlap for 2-night:
- Venue buyout = the exclusive-use fee the couple commits to
- Guest rooms = the per-room revenue from guests actually booking

The buyout model works like this:
1. Couple commits to the buyout (119M for 2 nights)
2. Guest room bookings (also 119M if all 17 fill at 3.5M/night × 2) offset the buyout
3. If rooms fill → venue is effectively "free" for the couple, they only pay F&B out of pocket
4. **Oshom carries zero risk** — the couple guarantees the buyout regardless

**Key question:** Is the venue buyout price SEPARATE from room revenue, or does room revenue offset it? The page says rooms offset the buyout, which means Oshom effectively gets paid twice (buyout guarantee + room revenue). Need to clarify the exact flow of money.

### Thinking

- 80 guests × 1,600,000 = **128,000,000 IDR sell** / **108,800,000 IDR buy**
- F&B is the **same for 2-night and 3-night** (wedding reception is one event regardless)
- Premium and Luxury tiers are placeholder — "coming soon"
