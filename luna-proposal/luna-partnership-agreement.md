## 1. Purpose

This agreement establishes a partnership between Bali Love and Luna Beach Club for the purpose of bringing destination wedding couples and their guests to Luna Beach Club.

This is an expression of mutual intent to work well together. It is not an employment contract, agency agreement, or exclusivity arrangement. Both parties enter this partnership in good faith with the shared goal of delivering exceptional experiences for wedding couples and generating revenue for Luna Beach Club.

## 2. What Bali Love Does

Bali Love will, at no upfront cost to Luna Beach Club:

- **Market Luna Beach Club** as a wedding destination through paid advertising (Google, Meta, TikTok), landing pages, SEO, and content creation — all advertising costs are borne entirely by Bali Love
- **Generate and convert leads** — handle all enquiries, sales calls, quoting, and follow-ups
- **Plan and coordinate weddings** — timelines, run sheets, styling, photography, videography, vendor management, and guest communications
- **Manage guest experiences** — travel concierge, accommodation coordination, activity recommendations, and dining referrals
- **Refer wedding-adjacent bookings** — welcome dinners, recovery brunches, bucks & hens, and guest dining throughout the week
- **Build and maintain landing pages** — dedicated wedding landing pages featuring Luna Beach Club, owned by Bali Love and built collaboratively with Luna Beach Club

All of this is provided at no charge to Luna Beach Club. Bali Love earns commission only when revenue is delivered.

## 3. What Luna Beach Club Does

Luna Beach Club will:

- **Deliver food, beverage, and venue hospitality** for all booked events to the standard expected of the venue
- **Designate a point of contact** for Bali Love's planning team to coordinate event logistics
- **Respond promptly** to enquiries, site tour requests, and planning questions — timely communication is essential to converting bookings and delivering great events
- **Provide product information** — menus, capacity guides, floor plans, AV specs, and any materials needed to sell and plan events effectively
- **Accept or decline bookings** within a reasonable timeframe when confirmations are sent through the Bali Love platform
- **Provide API access** to Luna Beach Club's point-of-sale system for the purpose of product, pricing, and availability synchronisation with the Bali Love platform
- **Provide brand assets** — logos, venue photography, and brand guidelines for use in marketing materials, subject to Luna Beach Club's approval of advertising creative before publication

## 4. Commission

- Bali Love earns a commission of **10%** on all Luna Beach Club products booked through the partnership, including food & beverage packages, venue buyouts, beverage packages, and any other Luna Beach Club products or services
- Commission is calculated as the difference between the couple-facing price and the amount remitted to Luna Beach Club — it is built into the pricing, not invoiced separately
- There are no retainers, setup fees, marketing fees, or any other charges to Luna Beach Club

## 5. Payment Flow

Each event is booked individually through the Bali Love platform. The payment process for each booking is:

1. **Couple pays Bali Love** — deposits and milestone payments are collected by Bali Love in the couple's preferred currency, according to the event contract payment schedule
2. **Bali Love confirms with Luna Beach Club** — a booking confirmation is sent to Luna Beach Club with event details
3. **Luna Beach Club accepts** — confirms the booking and issues an official invoice through the Bali Love platform
4. **Bali Love remits payment to Luna Beach Club** — within **5 business days** of Bali Love receiving the couple's payment and Luna Beach Club's signed invoice, Luna Beach Club receives their portion (total less commission) in **Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)**

Each event has its own payment schedule (typically: 25% deposit on booking, 25% at 12 months, 50% final balance at 30 days before the event). Luna Beach Club is paid at each milestone as funds are received.

## 6. Individual Event Contracts

This partnership agreement governs the relationship between Bali Love and Luna Beach Club. It does not govern individual events.

Each wedding or event is booked as its own separate contract, which defines:
- Event date, times, and spaces used
- F&B packages and pricing
- Payment schedule and amounts
- Cancellation and refund terms
- Specific responsibilities for that event

Event contracts are managed through the Bali Love platform and are agreed on a per-booking basis.

## 7. Marketing and Advertising

- Bali Love funds all advertising and marketing costs — Luna Beach Club has no cost exposure
- Bali Love may use Luna Beach Club's name, logo, venue photography, and brand assets in advertising and marketing materials
- **Luna Beach Club approves all advertising creative** before publication — Bali Love will submit creative for approval and Luna Beach Club will respond within a reasonable timeframe
- Bali Love owns all landing pages, advertising accounts, and marketing materials it creates
- On termination, Luna Beach Club will remove any links or embeds on its website within 30 days

## 8. Technology and Data

- Luna Beach Club provides Bali Love with API access to its point-of-sale system for the purpose of synchronising products, pricing, and availability
- Both parties share relevant data (lead information, booking details, guest preferences) as needed to deliver excellent customer experiences
- Guest data is collected with guest consent and both parties may access data relevant to their operations
- Both parties will handle all personal data in accordance with applicable privacy laws

## 9. How It Starts

This agreement takes effect on the date signed. Bali Love will begin:
- Onboarding Luna Beach Club into the platform (venue profile, products, pricing)
- Connecting to Luna Beach Club's POS system via API
- Building marketing materials and landing pages
- Referring qualified couples from the existing pipeline
- Integrating Luna Beach Club into guest communications and dining recommendations

There is no minimum commitment from either party. The partnership succeeds when both sides invest in making it work — responsiveness, quality, and a genuine desire to deliver great outcomes for couples.

## 10. How It Continues

This agreement runs indefinitely. There is no fixed term.

Both parties are encouraged to check in regularly to discuss what's working, what could improve, and how to grow the partnership. Pricing and product offerings can be adjusted at any time by mutual agreement.

## 11. How It Ends

Either party may end this partnership by giving **90 days' written notice** to the other party.

**On termination:**

- No new bookings will be made after the notice date
- **All confirmed event contracts at the time of notice will be honoured in full** — both parties will fulfil their obligations for every booked event, regardless of when the event takes place
- Bali Love will continue to plan and coordinate all confirmed events through to completion
- Luna Beach Club will continue to deliver F&B and venue services for all confirmed events
- Commission continues to be paid on all honoured events as per the normal payment flow
- Luna Beach Club will remove any Bali Love links or embeds from its website within 30 days of the partnership end date
- Bali Love will wind down advertising featuring Luna Beach Club within 30 days of the partnership end date

The wind-down ensures no couple is ever left without support. Every confirmed booking is delivered to the same standard, even if the partnership has ended.

## 12. General

- **Non-exclusive:** Both parties are free to work with other partners. This is not an exclusivity arrangement.
- **Insurance:** Each party maintains their own appropriate insurance coverage.
- **Independent parties:** Neither party is an employee, agent, or representative of the other (except where Bali Love acts as agent for couples in individual event contracts).
- **Confidentiality:** Both parties will keep commercially sensitive information (pricing, commission rates, financial arrangements, and client details) confidential and not share with competitors or third parties.
- **Governing law:** This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Indonesia.

## 13. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute arising from this agreement:

1. **Good faith discussion** — Both parties will first attempt to resolve the matter through direct discussion within 30 days
2. **Mediation** — If not resolved, the dispute will be referred to formal mediation in Bali, with costs shared equally between the parties
3. **Arbitration** — If mediation fails, the dispute will be referred to arbitration administered by BANI (Badan Arbitrase Nasional Indonesia) in Bali, conducted in English, with the decision binding on both parties

## 14. Values

This partnership works because both teams care about the same thing: creating unforgettable experiences for couples and their guests.

We believe the best partnerships are built on:
- **Responsiveness** — answering quickly, following through on commitments
- **Transparency** — honest communication about what's working and what isn't
- **Long-term thinking** — investing in the relationship, not just individual transactions
- **Shared pride** — both teams feeling like one team, producing something genuinely great together
