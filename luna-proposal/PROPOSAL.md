# Luna Beach Club Partnership Proposal

## Executive Summary

Bali Love proposes a partnership with Luna Beach Club to build a world-class wedding program together. We bring proven systems, dedicated team, and marketing infrastructure. Luna brings a stunning venue and exceptional F&B. Together, we create something neither could build alone.

This proposal outlines what we'd bring, how we'd work together, and what we'd expect to achieve.

---

## The Opportunity

Luna Beach Club has natural advantages for weddings:
- Iconic beachfront location
- Strong F&B reputation
- Beautiful event spaces
- Established brand

What's missing is the **engine** to consistently fill the calendar - the sales pipeline, marketing machine, and operational systems that turn interest into bookings.

That's what Bali Love does.

## Revenue Model

### Planning Fee
- Bali Love charges couples a planning fee (separate from Luna pricing)
- This covers our sales, marketing, and coordination costs
- Luna's pricing remains Luna's

### Commission on Luna Products
- Bali Love receives 10% commission on F&B and venue fees
- Luna retains 90% of all Luna revenue
- Aligns our incentives - we succeed when Luna succeeds

### Vendor Services
- Bali Love coordinates styling, photography, etc.
- These are separate vendor arrangements
- Luna not involved in vendor margins

## Illustrative Financials

*These numbers are illustrative based on our model. Actual results depend on execution and market conditions.*

### Assumptions (Base Scenario):
- 8 weddings/month (96/year)
- 80 guests average
- 25M IDR planning fee
- 10% commission on Luna F&B and venue

### Luna Revenue (Per Wedding):
| Item | Amount (IDR) | Luna Keeps (90%) |
|------|--------------|------------------|
| F&B (~1.5M × 80 guests) | 120,000,000 | 108,000,000 |
| Venue buyout | 50,000,000 | 45,000,000 |
| **Total** | **170,000,000** | **153,000,000** |

### Annual Luna Revenue (Base):
- 96 weddings × 153M IDR = **14.7B IDR** (~A$1.4M)

### Bali Love Revenue (Base):
- Planning fees: ~A$220K
- Commission (10%): ~A$130K
- Vendor margins: ~A$18K
- **Total: ~A$370K**

### Bali Love Costs:
- Staff (dedicated coordinators, sales): ~A$36K
- Marketing (ads, content, tools): ~A$24K
- Operations: ~A$6K
- **Total: ~A$66K**
