## Wedding Cost Modeling

### Typical Wedding at Luna (80 guests, Elysium ceremony + Beer Garden reception)

| Item | IDR | AUD (approx) |
|------|-----|--------------|
| Elysium buyout (full day) | 20,000,000 | ~1,800 |
| Indonesian 3-course dinner × 80 | 52,000,000 | ~4,700 |
| Free flow alcohol 3hr × 80 | 80,000,000 | ~7,300 |
| **F&B + Venue subtotal** | **152,000,000** | **~13,800** |
| Tax & service (23.2%) | 35,264,000 | ~3,200 |
| **Total (excl. extras)** | **187,264,000** | **~17,000** |

### Upscale Wedding (80 guests, Beer Garden buyout + Japanese menu)

| Item | IDR | AUD (approx) |
|------|-----|--------------|
| Beer Garden buyout (full day, F&B credit) | 300,000,000 | ~27,300 |
| Japanese degustation × 80 (from credit) | 52,000,000 | ~4,700 |
| Free flow alcohol 3hr × 80 (from credit) | 80,000,000 | ~7,300 |
| Remaining F&B credit to use | 123,000,000 | ~11,200 |
| Tax & service (23.2%) | 69,600,000 | ~6,300 |
| **Total** | **369,600,000** | **~33,600** |

### Key Pricing Observations
1. **Elysium is remarkably affordable** — IDR 20M full day (~A$1,800) for a clifftop, air-conditioned, ocean-view wedding venue
2. **Beer Garden buyout is premium** — IDR 300M but 85% credits to F&B, so effective rental is only IDR 45M
3. **F&B is mid-range for Bali luxury** — IDR 500K–700K/pax for food, IDR 790K–1M/pax for drinks
4. **Tax adds significantly** — 23.2% on everything (16.2% gov tax + 7% service)
5. **No planning/coordination fees listed** — This is where our platform adds value

### Opportunities
1. **Planning fee gap** — Luna has no wedding planning service. Our platform + coordination fills this.
2. **Volume capacity** — Multiple spaces mean parallel events are possible (ceremony in Elysium, reception in Beer Garden, pre-wedding at Main Stage).
3. **Diverse price points** — Can serve budget weddings (Elysium + Indonesian menu ~A$17K) through luxury (Beer Garden buyout ~A$34K+).
4. **Strong team in place** — 6-person sales/events team already exists; our platform augments, not replaces.
