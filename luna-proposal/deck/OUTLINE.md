# Luna Partnership Deck Outline

## Slide Structure (8-10 slides)

### 1. Title
- "Luna × Bali Love"
- "A Wedding Partnership Proposal"

### 2. The Opportunity
- Luna's natural advantages (venue, F&B, brand)
- What's missing: the engine (sales, marketing, operations)
- Together: something neither could build alone

### 3. What Bali Love Brings
- 300+ weddings delivered
- Sales & marketing infrastructure
- Dedicated team
- Technology platform
- **Distribution network** — 240 booked weddings, 15,000+ past guests, cross-event exposure
- Visual: Platform screenshots or process diagram
- Key message: "Join a pipeline, not just a platform"

### 4. How It Works
- Two columns: "Bali Love Handles" | "Luna Provides"
- Simple, clear division of responsibilities
- Visual: Partnership diagram

### 5. The Model
- Planning fee (Bali Love charges couples)
- 10% commission on Luna products (F&B, venue)
- Luna keeps 90% of Luna revenue
- "We succeed when Luna succeeds"

### 6. What We'd Implement
- Phase 1: Foundation (landing page, campaigns, process)
- Phase 2: Ramp (lead flow, pipeline building)
- Phase 3: Optimize (efficiency, refinement)
- Timeline visual

### 7. What We'd Expect
- NOT guarantees - projections based on execution
- Conservative / Base / Optimistic scenarios
- Key variables that drive success
- Table showing 50-120 weddings/year range

### 8. Illustrative Numbers
- Luna revenue per wedding: 153M IDR (after commission)
- Annual potential: 14.7B IDR at base scenario
- "Actual results depend on execution"

### 9. Why This Works
- For Luna: Revenue without operational burden
- For Bali Love: Iconic venue to offer
- For Couples: Seamless experience
- Aligned incentives

### 10. Next Steps
- Discussion → Terms → Soft Launch → Review
- "Let's talk"

---

## Design Notes

- Keep it visual, minimal text
- Luna branding colors where appropriate
- Photos of Luna venue if available
- Professional but warm tone
- No over-promising

## Key Messages

1. **Partnership, not takeover** - We work together
2. **Capabilities, not promises** - Here's what we bring
3. **Projections, not guarantees** - Based on execution
4. **Aligned incentives** - We win when Luna wins
