# Luna Beach Club — Partnership Kick-Off Plan

**Status:** Luna has agreed to the partnership. This document outlines the kick-off process, responsibilities, and timeline to go live.

**Target:** First bookings within 6-8 weeks of kick-off.

---

## Pre-Kick-Off (Week 0) — Before the First Meeting

Before sitting down with Luna, get these ready:

- [ ] Finalise partnership agreement (adapt template with Luna-specific terms)
- [ ] Confirm commission rate (10% as proposed, or negotiated)
- [ ] Confirm payment terms and flow
- [ ] Prepare onboarding checklist to share at kick-off meeting
- [ ] Identify Luna's key contact(s) — events manager, F&B lead, GM

**Owner:** Tom

---

## Kick-Off Meeting (Week 1)

### Agenda
1. **Celebrate** — acknowledge the partnership, set the tone
2. **Walk through the agreement** — confirm terms, answer questions, sign
3. **Introduce the onboarding process** — what we need from them, what they can expect from us
4. **Set communication rhythm** — weekly check-in for first 3 months
5. **Agree on immediate next steps** — who does what by when

### Deliverable
- Signed partnership agreement
- Luna point of contact confirmed
- Shared WhatsApp group / comms channel set up
- First onboarding call scheduled (within 3 days)

### Activities:

## Communication Cadence

| Cadence | What | Who |
|---------|------|-----|
| Daily (as needed) | Booking confirmations, logistics, questions | BL coordinator ↔ Luna contact |
| Weekly (first 3 months) | Pipeline update, upcoming tours, feedback | Tom ↔ Luna contact |
| Monthly | Performance review — bookings, revenue, leads | Tom ↔ Luna GM/leadership |
| Quarterly | Strategic review — what's working, what to adjust, growth plans | Leadership both sides |

## Risk & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Slow lead generation early on | Leverage existing 240-couple pipeline immediately |
| Luna responsiveness to enquiries | Set SLA expectations in kick-off (24hr response) |
| Quality gap in first weddings | BL team does thorough site walkthrough, rehearsal coordination |
| Seasonal slowdowns | Plan marketing calendar around peak booking seasons |
| Misaligned expectations on volume | Set clear monthly targets, review at 3-month mark |
