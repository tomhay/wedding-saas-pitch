## Key Takeaways for Pricing

- **2025 actual room revenue: 9.47B IDR** — this is the baseline we're comparing against
- **2026 budget room revenue: 16.1B IDR** — what Oshom expects without weddings
- **2025 avg occupancy: 47.4%** — massive room for improvement
- **2026 budget occupancy: 61%** — still 39% empty
- **Avg room rate 2025: 3.31M IDR** — our proposed 3.5M sell / 2.975M buy is in range
- **Avg room rate 2026 budget: 4.03M IDR** — their budget expects higher rates than our buy price
- **Banquet revenue is tiny** — only 877M in 2025. Wedding F&B would be transformative
- **Weakest months** (Nov, Mar, Apr) = exactly when weddings book
