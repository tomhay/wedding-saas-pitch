## Key Observations

1. **Accommodation buyout is 153M/night net** — that's 8.5M/room/night for 18 rooms. Our proposed sell of 3.5M/room/night is **59% below their published buyout rate per room.**
2. **But** the published rate includes breakfast, afternoon tea, turndown etc — it's a full luxury service. Our rate is a stripped-back wedding buyout.
3. **Venue buyout is 100M/day net on top** — we don't have a separate venue fee in our current model.
4. **Food ranges from 400K to 850K net** before 21% tax. With tax: 484K to 1,028K. Our proposed 700K/head is within this range.
5. **Drinks range from 400K to 750K net** before tax. With tax: 484K to 907K. Our proposed 900K/head is at the top of their range.
6. **The wedding packages (89M-185M)** are ceremony/decoration packages and DON'T include venue rental or accommodation.
7. **Total 21% tax applies to everything** — this is a major factor we need to account for in our pricing model.
