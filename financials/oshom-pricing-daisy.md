## 3. How the Pricing Works

### The Pricing Chain

Every product follows the same structure:

```
Oshom net price (what Oshom keeps after tax)
  × 1.21 (Oshom's 21% tax & service charge)
  = Buy price (what Bali Love pays Oshom)
  ÷ 0.85 (Bali Love's 15% commission)
  = Sell price (what the couple pays)
```

Bali Love's **15% commission** covers all marketing, sales, coordination, and technology. Oshom receives the buy price (inclusive of tax) for every product.

- **Increase average wedding value** from $35K toward $40K+ over time
