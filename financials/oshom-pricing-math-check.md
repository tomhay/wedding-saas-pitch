# Oshom Pricing — Math Check

## The Chain

```
Oshom net price (what they keep after tax)
  × 1.21 (their 21% tax/service)
  = BUY PRICE (what BaliLove pays Oshom)
  ÷ 0.85 (add 15% BL commission)
  = SELL PRICE (what couple pays)
```

Total markup on Oshom's net: × 1.21 ÷ 0.85 = **× 1.4235 (42.35% total markup)**

---

## Accommodation Buyout — Worked Example

**Starting point:** What net rate per room per night should Oshom receive?

Their actual ADR data:
- 2025 actual: 3,310,000 /room/night (at 47% occupancy)
- 2026 budget: 4,028,000 /room/night (at 61% occupancy)

For weddings we deliver 100% occupancy, so a lower per-room rate is justified. Let's try **2,500,000 net per room/night.**

### 2-Night Buyout

| Step | Calculation | Amount |
|------|------------|--------|
| Oshom net (per room/night) | — | 2,500,000 |
| Oshom net (18 rooms × 2 nights) | 18 × 2 × 2,500,000 | **90,000,000** |
| + 21% tax | 90,000,000 × 1.21 | **108,900,000** (buy) |
| + 15% BL commission | 108,900,000 ÷ 0.85 | **128,118,000** (sell) |
| BL earns | 128,118,000 − 108,900,000 | **19,218,000** |

**Check:** BL commission ÷ sell = 19,218,000 ÷ 128,118,000 = **15.0%** ✓
**Oshom revenue per night** (incl tax): 108,900,000 ÷ 2 = **54,450,000**
**vs their 2026 budget per night:** 44,200,000 → **+23% above budget** ✓

### 3-Night Buyout

Slight discount per night to incentivize longer stays: **2,250,000 net per room/night**

| Step | Calculation | Amount |
|------|------------|--------|
| Oshom net (per room/night) | — | 2,250,000 |
| Oshom net (18 rooms × 3 nights) | 18 × 3 × 2,250,000 | **121,500,000** |
| + 21% tax | 121,500,000 × 1.21 | **147,015,000** (buy) |
| + 15% BL commission | 147,015,000 ÷ 0.85 | **172,959,000** (sell) |
| BL earns | 172,959,000 − 147,015,000 | **25,959,000** |

**Oshom revenue per night** (incl tax): 147,015,000 ÷ 3 = **49,005,000**
**vs budget:** +11% above ✓
