## The Couple's Perspective (What They Actually Pay Out of Pocket)

Remember: guest room bookings offset the buyout. If 17 rooms fill:

Guest room revenue: 17 rooms × sell rate per room/night × nights

We need a guest room sell rate. If the buyout sell is 127M for 2 nights for 18 rooms:
- Per room/night sell = 127,060,000 / 18 / 2 = **3,530,000**
- 17 guest rooms × 3,530,000 × 2 nights = **120,020,000** offset

| Scenario (2-night, base) | Amount |
|--------------------------|--------|
| Total couple pays (sell) | 230,682,000 |
| Less: guest room payments (17 rooms fill) | -120,020,000 |
| **Couple's net out-of-pocket** | **110,662,000** |
| **= ~$10,430 AUD** | |

That's the couple's actual cost for a 2-night Bali wedding with 80 guests, cash bar, all accommodation — **~$10,400 AUD out of pocket.** That's incredibly competitive.

For 3-night standard package:
- Sell: 335,671,000
- Guest offset (17 × 3,530K × 3): -180,030,000
- **Couple net: ~155,641,000 = ~$14,680 AUD**
