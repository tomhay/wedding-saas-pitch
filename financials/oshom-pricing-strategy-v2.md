## Proposed Pricing (Revised)

### The Principle

Oshom's published buyout is 153M+100M = **253M net/night**. Nobody is paying that for a wedding. Our job is to find a price that:

1. **Beats their actual revenue** per night (not their published rate)
2. **Feels right** to Daisy / doesn't devalue the brand
3. **Competes** with Vedas, Elite Haven, Glampnusa
4. **Gives couples** a compelling reason to choose Oshom

### Product 1: Full Buyout (Accommodation + Venue)

The couple gets the whole hotel — all 18 rooms + all event spaces — for 2 or 3 nights.

**Option A: Keep current pricing**

| Package | Sell (Couple Pays) | Buy (Oshom Keeps) | Per Room/Night (Buy) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 2-Night | 119,000,000 | 101,150,000 | 2,808,000 |
| 3-Night | 154,000,000 | 130,900,000 | 2,425,000 |

- Pros: Low price = easy to sell, high volume
- Cons: 2.8M/room is below their 2025 actual ADR (3.3M), Selina says 3.5M is already ceiling

**Option B: Price at their 2025 actual ADR level**

| Package | Sell (Couple Pays) | Buy (Oshom Keeps) | Per Room/Night (Buy) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 2-Night | 140,000,000 | 119,000,000 | 3,306,000 |
| 3-Night | 185,000,000 | 157,250,000 | 2,913,000 |

- Pros: Matches what they actually earned in 2025, feels fair
- Cons: May still feel low vs their 2026 budget ambitions

**Option C: Price to beat their actual nightly revenue (the "you earn more" argument)**

Their best revenue per night (2026 budget): 18 rooms × 61% × 4.03M = **44.2M**
Our buyout at 50.6M buy/night (Option A) or 59.5M/night (Option B) already beats this.

**Recommendation:** Go with something close to Option A but frame it differently. The per-room rate doesn't matter — what matters is **total revenue per night vs what they'd otherwise earn.** A wedding night at our current pricing delivers 50.6M to Oshom vs 44.2M on a normal night. That's the argument.

### Product 2: Food & Beverage

Based on Oshom's actual menu pricing and Selina's feedback:

**Proposed F&B tiers:**

| Tier | Food/Head | Drinks/Head | Total/Head (Sell) | Total/Head (Buy) | 80 Guests (Buy) |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Base (cash bar)** | 500,000 | Cash bar | 500,000 | 425,000 | 34,000,000 |
| **Standard** | 600,000 | 550,000 (Silver) | 1,150,000 | 977,500 | 78,200,000 |
| **Premium** | 850,000 | 650,000 (Gold) | 1,500,000 | 1,275,000 | 102,000,000 |

**Key changes from v1:**
- Added **cash bar option** (Selina's #1 recommendation) — food only, guests buy their own drinks
- Base food at 500K matches Oshom's actual Balinese buffet pricing (400K net + tax ≈ 484K)
- Standard matches their Family Style (600K net)
- Premium matches their BBQ/Western (850K net)
- Drinks tiers match Oshom's actual packages

**Cash bar mechanics:**
- Couple pays for food package only
- Bar runs on guest's own tab (Oshom's bar prices)
- Removes the biggest friction point (Selina: "no-brainer")
- Oshom still earns bar revenue — just paid by guests, not couple

### Product 3: No Minimum Spend

Per Selina's feedback: **remove the minimum spend requirement entirely.**

- The buyout IS the commitment — couple has already guaranteed the accommodation
- F&B minimum should be effortlessly achievable (e.g. 80 guests × 500K base = 40M... but don't frame it as a "minimum")
- Just price the food per head and let it scale naturally
- This removes the friction that kills deals at Vedas (40M/night minimum)

## Open Questions

1. **Do we include the 21% tax in our pricing or work on net?** Oshom's published rates are net + 21%. If we sell at 119M and that's inclusive, Oshom only gets ~98M after tax. Need to clarify.
2. **Corkage for cash bar?** If guests buy drinks at bar prices, that's fine. But do we need to account for pre/post event drinks? Villas let guests bring their own — Oshom won't.
3. **3-night buyout pricing** — current 154M sell seems low for 3 nights. That's only 2.85M/room/night sell. Should we push this up?
4. **Wedding ceremony package** — is this included in our buyout or does Oshom charge separately (42-185M for ceremony/decoration)?
5. **How do we handle the venue buyout (100M/day)?** In our model the couple gets the whole hotel. Does Oshom expect a separate venue fee on top of accommodation?
