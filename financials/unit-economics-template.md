## Key Assumptions to Validate
1. Commission rate acceptable to venues (vs. 15-20% to planners)
2. Wedding volume per venue accurate
3. Guest room booking capture rate
4. Venue churn rate
5. Sales cycle length
6. Onboarding time and cost
