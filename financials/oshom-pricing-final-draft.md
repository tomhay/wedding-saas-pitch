## 1. How the Pricing Chain Works

Every product follows the same chain:

```
Step 1: Oshom net price        (what Oshom keeps after tax)
Step 2: × 1.21                 (Oshom's 21% tax & service charge)
Step 3: = BUY price            (what BaliLove pays Oshom)
Step 4: ÷ 0.85                 (add BaliLove's 15% commission)
Step 5: = SELL price            (what the couple pays)
```

**Total markup on Oshom net: × 1.4235 (42.35%)**

Example: If Oshom nets 1,000,000 → Buy = 1,210,000 → Sell = 1,423,529

## 5. The Core Tension

We need a price that satisfies three parties:

| Party | What They Want |
|-------|---------------|
| **Daisy (Oshom)** | At or above her 2026 budget ADR of 4,028,398 /room/night |
| **Selina (Sales)** | Guest room rate at or below 3,500,000 /room/night sell |
| **Couple** | Total out-of-pocket comparable to or better than Vedas (~217M - 265M) |

**Solution: Decouple the buyout rate from the guest room rate.**

- Buyout rate = what Oshom receives per room (based on ADR) → **satisfies Daisy**
- Guest room rate = what guests pay per room (based on market) → **satisfies Selina**
- The gap between the two = the couple's "exclusivity premium" → **keeps couple competitive with Vedas**

## 15. Resolved & Remaining Questions

### Resolved

1. **Venue fee** — Rolled into buyout in §16 pricing. No separate venue charge. *(In §6–14 it was 40M buy as a separate line — superseded.)*

2. **BYO alcohol / Corkage** — No corkage. Guests can bring their own and we'll serve it, but with a reasonable limit.

3. **Non-wedding day F&B** — Guests order from Oshom's menu/bar on non-event days. Aim to upsell functions (welcome dinner, recovery brunch) but no mandatory F&B on non-event days. This revenue goes to Oshom directly. *(Note: We don't do cash bars — all F&B tiers include drinks packages.)*

4. **Guest room rate discounting** — We'd suggest to Daisy that off-peak dates should have discounted guest room rates. Look at which months are struggling (Nov, Apr, Mar) and price guest rooms lower to drive bookings. This makes weddings more attractive in weak months and helps fill the calendar.

### Still Open

1. **Guest room rate of 3,500,000 sell** — is this the standard rate to present, with discounting available for off-peak? Or should we set a range (e.g. 2,500,000 - 3,500,000 depending on season)?
